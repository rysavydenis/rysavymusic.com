About Rysavy


Hello nice to meet you

My Name is Denis Rysavy.

I am the Son of Arife Demirer and Dr. Thomas Rysavy .

I was born on January 16 ,1996 in Gießen, Germany.

I am a Music Producer and Programmer.

I am making electronic Music since 2015. Before I was playing the bass guitar.

I enjoyed playing in a Band ( we were mostly covering songs by Jimmy Hendrix ).

I started going to rave Parties and got addicted to the unlimited possibilities of creating all kind of sounds with a DAW and a computer.

After graduating from school, it was time for me to move on and leave my parents home.

After moving from the Village (i was living with my parents in Oberdünzebach, Eschwege) to Bremen in Oktober 2015 I was ready for the next steps.

I started exploring the World of Psytrance more and more.

In 2017, I released my debut album Meaninglessness at Multifrequency Records.

This was the Kick off and soon my first Live Gigs were happening. Now in 2019 I am member of Minuteless Recordings.
Preparing the next events like : Minuteless Debut Album, Live Gig in Brazil, and much more ...
